Until few years ago, TOEIC was go-to exam for anyone trying to get an english certification.
However, as the importance of English speaking and everyday communication have been emphasized, speaking tests like TOEIC speaking and OPIc have started to gain popularity.

I also had to prepare for the OPIc in a very short amount of time to get a job.
I faced a few challenges while studying for OPIc. 
It covers a wide rage of topics, but I feel like my own experiences are too limited to answer them all. I'd like to share how I solve it for OPIc in such a short period of time.

Preparing for the OPIc in a short time-especially when you feel you lack specific personal experiences is actually common challenge. The good news is that  OPIC doesn't grade you on `truth or the uniqueness of your life story`; it grades your ability to maintain a natural flow of conversation.
